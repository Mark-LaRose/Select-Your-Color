Select or

This application is perfect for helping you choose colors for your projects. You can generate random colors, select a specific color using the color picker, and save your favorite colors for future reference. Click on a favorite color to view it again, or remove it from the list if you no longer need it. Also you can drag and drop your favorites to rearrange their order.

Each color displays its hex code, and within the selector you can view the RGB and HSL values as well for later reference. Additionally, you can use the color picker to sample colors from other images. Have fun exploring and finding the perfect colors for your needs!
